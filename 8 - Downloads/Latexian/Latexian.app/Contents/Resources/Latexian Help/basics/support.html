If you are having problems with Latexian, or you would just like to offer some feedback to Taco Software, then Taco Software offers email support. Before contacting Taco Software, please check the FAQs and the Latexian Help Documentation to see if your question is addressed there. If you are unable to find an answer, don't hesitate to contact us at support@tacosw.com for assistance.
